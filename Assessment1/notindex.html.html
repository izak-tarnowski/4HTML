A little info

Izak Tarnowski (me!) was born in Alice Springs, 8th of January, 1999. He moved to Perth, Western Australia at about 6 months old and grew up there. Attending Newton Primary School from 2003 to 2010, from Kindergarten to Year 6 he was a little shitbag. Acing all primary school exercises throughout, he was moved to Seton Catholic College for Year 7 to Year 9. Come to 2014, and Izak moved back to his birthplace, Alice Springs. Sonny publicly moved to Electronic Dance Music in roughly 2009, with his first few remixes;
  • Bad Romance by Lada Gaga
  • What is Light Where is Laughter by Twin Atlantic.

He has since continued this trend of EDM, with the creation of multiple groups (eg Jack Ü) and collaboration with big names (eg Damian "Junior Gong" Marley, A$AP Ferg).
